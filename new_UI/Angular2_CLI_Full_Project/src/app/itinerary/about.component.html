What is ChatIT?

How often has it been the case where you want to travel to a location but have no idea where to start? Even if you start you end up opening thousands of tabs.
ChatIT is a Social Itinerary Application which provides an easy and social alternative to build up a travel itinerary for any location you choose. We bring passionate travelers who want to share their experiences on a platform and also allow others to go out into the world and explore.

Lets explore the unexplored beautiful places brought up by the experiences of previous travelers and travel like a local!
